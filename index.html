Our Services
Who are we?

Clearmission Nigeria Limited is a leading media and publicity company, offering an integrated range of services that include Publishing, Media & content production, Events Management, Branding, Graphic Design, Web Development, Packaging and Large Format & General Printing. We are strongly committed to quick and quality delivery. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in a variety of high quality services and products including wall murals, event graphics, trade-show displays, vehicle wraps, and window graphics, alongside our media, publishing and events offerings. Clearmission Nigeria Limited is an indigenous company incorporated in Nigeria on 17th December 2005 under the Company and Allied Matters Decree 1990. Our offices are strategically located within Lokoja City Center from where our numerous customers are promptly served with quality output. We are a well known leader in media, publicity, branding, advertising, publishing and events management. The top of the range machinery and creative talent, combined with a commitment to consistent quality delivery, ranks our company among the leading service providers in the industry.
