About us

Chief Executive Officer

SHUAIB ILESANMI IPINMISHO

Chief Executive Officer, Clearmission Nig Ltd

Our CEO is a seasoned entrepreneur and business leader with over two decades of experience in printing, publishing, branding, media, and business development. Passionate about innovation and community development, He has dedicated his career to building businesses that create value while empowering young people through skills development, mentorship, and employment opportunities.

With a vision to make Clearmission a leading name in media and publicity across Nigeria, Shuaib Ipinmisho has steered the company from a printing outfit into a full service media, publishing, and events management company. A hands-on leader, He works closely with every department to ensure clients receive creative work that is delivered on time and to a high standard. Passionate about sustainable growth, he remains committed to building lasting value for clients, partners, and the communities Clearmission serves.

Who are we?

Clearmission Nigeria Limited is a leading media and publicity company. We offer an integrated range of services including Publishing, Media & content production, Events Management, Branding, Graphic Design, Web Development, Packaging, Order & Delivery, and Large Format & General Printing. We are strongly committed to quick and quality delivery.
With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in a variety of high quality services and products including wall murals, event graphics, trade-show displays, vehicle wraps, and window graphics, alongside our media, publishing and events offerings.
Clearmission Nigeria Limited is an indigenous company incorporated in Nigeria on 17th December 2005 under the Company and Allied Matters Decree 1990. Our offices are strategically located within Lokoja City Center from where our numerous customers are promptly served with quality output.
We are a well known leader in media, publicity, branding, advertising, publishing and events management. The top of the range machinery and creative talent, combined with the commitment of our personnel to consistent quality delivery, ranks our company among the leading service providers in the industry.

Our Clients include international and local NGOs, Private and Government Institutions, religious bodies, health care and educational Institutions, Outdoor Advertising Agencies, Telecommunication Firms, Banks and Financial Institutions, Individuals and Printing Contractors.
Our Vision is to be the frontline media, publicity, advertising and publishing company in Nigeria and beyond. Our Mission is to provide prompt and quality media, publicity and printing services at competitive prices using cutting edge technology and highly skilled personnel.
We assure you of excellent services at all time as we welcome you to Clearmission – your access to quality media and publicity solutions.
